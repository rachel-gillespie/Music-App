David Guetta is a world-renowned French DJ and music producer who has been a dominant force in electronic dance music since the 1990s. 

Born in Paris in 1967, Guetta began his career playing in clubs before gaining international recognition for his hit albums like "Just a Little More Love" (2002) and "Pop Life" (2007). He's known for his innovative blend of house music with pop and urban influences, leading to collaborations with some of the biggest names in the industry, including Sia, Rihanna, and Nicki Minaj.

Guetta has achieved remarkable commercial success, selling over 10 million albums and 65 million singles worldwide. He was named the number one DJ in the DJ Mag Top 100 DJs poll in 2011, and again from 2020 to 2023.  His music has also been featured in numerous films and television shows, solidifying his status as a global icon in the electronic music scene.
