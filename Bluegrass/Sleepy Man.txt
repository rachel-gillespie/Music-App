Sleepy Man Banjo Boys are a bluegrass band from New Jersey, made up of brothers Jonny, Robbie, and Tommy Mizzone. They gained popularity through their YouTube videos, showcasing their impressive instrumental skills at a young age. Jonny, the youngest, is a banjo prodigy who has been praised by bluegrass legend J.D. Crowe. 

The brothers have released several albums and have performed on numerous television shows and at prestigious venues like the Grand Ole Opry and TED conferences. Their music is influenced by bluegrass masters like Earl Scruggs and Ralph Stanley, but they bring a youthful energy and their own unique style to the genre. 

While they've shortened their name to simply "Sleepy Man", their energetic performances and captivating bluegrass sound continue to charm audiences.
